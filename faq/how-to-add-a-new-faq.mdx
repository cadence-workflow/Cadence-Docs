FAQs are written based on community inquiries; if you have a question simply reach out to us using our GitHub org or Slack workspace/channels (see [our support page](https://cadenceworkflow.io/community/support)).

If this question comes up often in your company, recommend us adding it to our FAQ; otherwise, we will add new questions based on popularity we receive.
